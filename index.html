Trouble focusing in a virtual learning environment? We've all been there.

Refocus is designed to help you stay on task, whether it be a brief study session or an extensive zoom lecture. Through motion detection and facial recognition, Refocus will digitally assist you with every work session, reminding you to stay on task with both audio and visual cues. As well, you will receive a summary at the end of each session composed of data of your focus and moods.

More than just productivity, Refocus focuses on your wellbeing, and will verbally remind you to take water breaks! If you are in the mood for Spotify or a ten hour rain soundtrack, give Refocus a shot too. Life is difficult enough; make yours a little easier with Refocus =).
